Campus Life

School Church

was established in 1978

While Kendriya Vidyalaya is a secular institution, we understand the importance of spiritual growth and moral education. Our school chapel provides a serene environment for reflection and spiritual activities: Peaceful Atmosphere: The chapel offers a tranquil space for students and staff to reflect, meditate, and find peace amidst their busy schedules. Interfaith Services: Regular services and activities are conducted, respecting all religions and promoting a message of unity and harmony. Moral Education: Sessions on moral and ethical values are held, helping students to develop a strong moral compass and understanding of diverse religious beliefs. The chapel is a welcoming space for all students, providing support and guidance in their personal and spiritual growth.

school library

was build in 1980

Our state-of-the-art library is the heart of Kendriya Vidyalaya’s academic environment, providing a vast collection of resources to support the educational needs of our students and staff. The library features: Extensive Collection: Over 10,000 books including fiction, non-fiction, academic texts, and reference materials. Digital Resources: Access to online databases, e-books, and academic journals to facilitate research and learning. Reading Area: Comfortable and quiet reading spaces designed to encourage a love for reading and self-study among students. Computer Stations: Equipped with the latest technology to assist with research, assignments, and project work. Librarian Assistance: Our knowledgeable librarians are always available to help students find the resources they need and to guide them in their academic pursuits. The library regularly hosts book fairs, reading challenges, and author visits to promote literacy and a love of reading within the school community.

school auditorium

was build in 1982

School Auditorium The auditorium at Kendriya Vidyalaya is a hub of cultural and extracurricular activities, designed to host a variety of events: Seating Capacity: Accommodates up to 500 people, making it ideal for school assemblies, performances, and guest lectures. Advanced Audio-Visual Equipment: State-of-the-art sound and lighting systems to enhance the experience of performances, presentations, and screenings. Versatile Stage: A spacious stage suitable for plays, musicals, dance performances, and debates. Events and Activities: The auditorium hosts an array of events including annual day celebrations, talent shows, debates, seminars, and guest speaker sessions. The auditorium serves as a central venue for fostering students’ talents and enhancing their extracurricular experiences, encouraging them to showcase their skills and creativity.
